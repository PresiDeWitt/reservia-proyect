## 📋 Modelos detallados

> [!info] `Restaurant` — Restaurante
>
> | Campo | Tipo Django | Tipo SQL | Restricciones |
> |---|---|---|---|
> | `id` | `BigAutoField` | INTEGER | PK · autoincrement |
> | `name` | `CharField` | VARCHAR(200) | NOT NULL |
> | `cuisine` | `CharField` | VARCHAR(100) | NOT NULL |
> | `location` | `CharField` | VARCHAR(200) | NOT NULL |
> | `distance` | `CharField` | VARCHAR(50) | NOT NULL |
> | `rating` | `FloatField` | REAL | NOT NULL |
> | `price_range` | `CharField` | VARCHAR(10) | NOT NULL |
> | `address` | `CharField` | VARCHAR(300) | NOT NULL |
> | `description` | `TextField` | TEXT | NOT NULL |
> | `lat` | `FloatField` | REAL | NOT NULL |
> | `lng` | `FloatField` | REAL | NOT NULL |
> | `image_url` | `URLField` | VARCHAR(500) | NOT NULL |
> | `reviews_count` | `IntegerField` | INTEGER | DEFAULT 0 |
>
> **Ordenación por defecto:** `ORDER BY rating DESC`

> [!info] `MenuItem` — Plato del menú
>
> | Campo | Tipo Django | Tipo SQL | Restricciones |
> |---|---|---|---|
> | `id` | `BigAutoField` | INTEGER | PK · autoincrement |
> | `restaurant_id` | `ForeignKey` | INTEGER | FK → `api_restaurant` · CASCADE |
> | `name` | `CharField` | VARCHAR(200) | NOT NULL |
> | `description` | `TextField` | TEXT | BLANK permitido |
> | `price` | `FloatField` | REAL | NOT NULL |
>
> **Relación:** N `MenuItem` → 1 `Restaurant` · `related_name='menu_items'`

> [!warning] `Reservation` — Reserva
>
> | Campo | Tipo Django | Tipo SQL | Restricciones |
> |---|---|---|---|
> | `id` | `BigAutoField` | INTEGER | PK · autoincrement |
> | `user_id` | `ForeignKey` | INTEGER | FK → `auth_user` · CASCADE |
> | `restaurant_id` | `ForeignKey` | INTEGER | FK → `api_restaurant` · CASCADE |
> | `date` | `DateField` | DATE | NOT NULL |
> | `time` | `TimeField` | TIME | NOT NULL |
> | `guests` | `IntegerField` | INTEGER | NOT NULL · validado 1–20 |
> | `status` | `CharField` | VARCHAR(20) | CHOICES: `confirmed` / `cancelled` · DEFAULT `confirmed` |
> | `created_at` | `DateTimeField` | DATETIME | auto_now_add=True |
>
> **Ordenación por defecto:** `ORDER BY date DESC, time DESC`
> **Relación:** N `Reservation` → 1 `User` · N `Reservation` → 1 `Restaurant`

> [!example] `UserProfile` — Perfil extendido del usuario
>
> | Campo | Tipo Django | Tipo SQL | Restricciones |
> |---|---|---|---|
> | `id` | `BigAutoField` | INTEGER | PK · autoincrement |
> | `user_id` | `OneToOneField` | INTEGER | FK → `auth_user` · CASCADE · UNIQUE |
> | `phone` | `CharField` | VARCHAR(30) | BLANK permitido |
>
> **Relación:** 1 `UserProfile` ↔ 1 `User` (one-to-one) · `related_name='profile'`

## 🔧 Configuración de motores

> [!tip] Lógica de selección en `settings.py`
>
> ```python
> if os.environ.get('DATABASE_URL'):
>     # Producción → PostgreSQL (Railway)
>     DATABASES = { 'default': dj_database_url.config(...) }
> else:
>     # Desarrollo → SQLite local
>     DATABASES = { 'default': { 'ENGINE': 'sqlite3', 'NAME': BASE_DIR / 'db.sqlite3' } }
> ```
>
> | Entorno | Motor | Ubicación | Persistencia |
> |---|---|---|---|
> | Desarrollo local | SQLite | `backend/db.sqlite3` | archivo local |
> | Docker local | SQLite | `/app/data/db.sqlite3` | volumen `db_data` |
> | Producción | PostgreSQL | Railway plugin | gestionado por Railway |
